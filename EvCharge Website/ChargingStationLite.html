EvCharge Self-Charging Station Lite


The Self-Charging Station Lite for Electric Vehicles is a 240V-40 Amp Level 2 Charger compatible with all EV. It features a sleek contemporary design in dark gray and is installable indoors or outdoors.

The Self-Charging Station Lite offers 2 Universal slots that provides the advantage of charging up to 2 batteries simultaneously with up to 40 Amps.
